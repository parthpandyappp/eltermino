General Instructions

  • The game comprises of 10 levels
  • Each level presents you an un_scrambled word, which needs to be scrambled up.
  • The word entered by you must be greater than or equal to 3 characters
  • You can only enter the characters present in the un_scrambled word
  • You will enter 5 words which when matches our dictionary lookup, passes you in that particular level and rewards you with a point.
  • If the word entered by you doesn't matches our dictionary lookup but still exist in the universal dictionary, then here you discovers an extra word. Per two extra-words you'll be offered 2 reward points.
  • If your word doesn't exist in our dictionary lookup and niether in the universal dictionary, you are flagged and the game's still continue
  • In the end, we provide you the definition for the actual & extra words
